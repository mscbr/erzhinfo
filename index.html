ABOUT


There is not much more behind this production process than just observation of appearing phenomena. Of course personal factor can't be completely taken out, but it can be controlled when taken into account. Palette of impressions derived from certain moments then transferred to synthesized sounds. Reflections of the moment.

ERZH comes from Krakow and is currently based in Reykjavik. Released by Boshke Beat Records, Nashton Records & FALK Records. Used to play at local venues in his hometown, now you can meet him performing at places around capital of Iceland. DJ sets under alias Mt. Acme will take you to lofi acid vibes and live acts bring the noise raving alley closer to your senses.
